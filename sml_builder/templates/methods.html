Codes and specification permission

  • Public methods’ are accessible to all users within and outside the organisations
  • Internal methods' are not sensitive but not publicly available yet. This may be due to methods undergoing development or review
  • Private methods’ contain sensitive information and restricted to approved researchers only. These methods also include author-restricted methods. There are certain requirements that need to be met before access is granted. See instructions on how to request access to private methods
